March 2017 Our commitment to privacy SitUpChair ASBL (hereafter « SitUpChair », « the company », « us », « we ») knows that you care how information about you is used and shared, and we appreciate you trusting that we will do so carefully and sensibly. To better protect your privacy, we provide this Privacy Policy explaining our practices and the choices you can make about the way your information is collected and used by SitUpChair. The information below explains our policy regarding your privacy, both online and offline. By visiting our website www.SitUpChair-belgique.be or sharing personal information with SitUpChair via any of its channels (website, chatbot…) you are accepting the practices described in this Privacy Notice. What personally identifiable information is collected by SitUpChair? When use our applications, or interact with our chatbot with the purpose of getting information about us, you may provide us with personal information (such as name, address, email address, telephone numbers and/or credit/debit card information) that you knowingly choose to disclose, which is collected on an individual basis for various purposes. These purposes include registering to receive email newsletters or other materials, requesting further information from us about projects and services, making requests, submitting a form on our website, or simply asking a question. We receive and store any information you enter on our website or give us in any other way. We ask for personal information so that we can fulfill your request and return your message. This information is retained and used in accordance with existing laws, rules, regulations, and other policies in force in the European Union. SitUpChair does not collect personal information from you unless you provide it to us. If you choose not to provide any of that information, we may not be able to fulfill your request, but you will still be free to browse the other sections of the websites owned and administered by us. This means that you can visit our site without telling us who you are or revealing any personally identifiable information about yourself. The way we use information When you supply information about yourself for a specific purpose, we use the information for only that purpose (such as to provide the service or information you have requested). For example, you may be asked to give us individual information to receive information, through your personal network. We do not share this information with outside parties except to the extent necessary to complete your request. Similarly, should you decide to share any of your friends’ data (names), it would only be used in the context of the intended request or experience within our site, chatbot or application. You can register with our website if you would like to receive updates on our new projects and services. Information you submit on our website, over the phone, or via our chatbot will not be used for this purpose unless a subscription is created via a form or in accordance with the platform’s policy. We use return email addresses to answer the email we receive. Such addresses are not used for any other purpose and are not shared with outside parties. SitUpChair does not sell, rent, give-away or share its email addresses or other personal contact information with outside sources. SitUpChair also does not send mailings on behalf of other organizations. Should any material changes be made to the ways in which we use personally identifiable information, SitUpChair will take commercially reasonable measures to obtain consent from you. We will also post the changes to our use of personally identifiable information on our website, applications or chatbots at least 30 days prior to a change. Our commitment to data security Personally identifiable information is stored on our server and is not publicly accessible. Further, personally identifiable information is only accessed by SitUpChair’s staff on a "need to know" basis. To prevent unauthorized access, maintain data accuracy, and ensure the correct use of information, we have put in place appropriate physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect online. Additionally, sensitive data such as debit card numbers are encrypted using SSL and other industry standard measures, to provide an additional level of security. Choice/opt-out If you have registered to receive communications from us and later change your mind, you may contact us to have your name and contact information removed from our distribution lists. You have the following options to do this: You can send an email to: info@SitUpChair-belgique.be You can send mail to the following address: SitUpChair – Boulevard Initialis, 1 – 700 Mons - Belgium Correct/update If you would like to verify the data we have received from you or to make corrections to it, you may contact us directly at the email and mail addresses provided above. In our chatbot, you may always access your personal settings by simply typing « settings » or by accessing the menu in order to change some of the data (such as location) you communicated us for the purpose of satisfying your request. What anonymous information is collected by SitUpChair? Anonymous information is collected for every visitor to this site. This includes pages viewed, date and time, and browser type. IP numbers are not stored, but are temporarily used to determine domain type and in some cases, geographic region. We do not make any association between this information and a visitor's identity. Automatic information When you visit our website, our servers make a log of basic information corresponding to the sites and pages you have visited. This information is stored primarily to track the effectiveness of our website and individual sections and pages within them. How does this site use cookies? Cookies are simple text files stored by your web browser that provide a method of distinguishing among visitors to the website. SitUpChair uses cookies to identify your browser as you visit pages on the SitUpChair website or sites in the SitUpChair media network. Cookies created on your computer by using our website do not contain personally identifiable information and do not compromise your privacy or security. Cookies allow SitUpChair to gather anonymous information. Cookies also allow SitUpChair to provide more relevant, targeted content as you travel through sites in the SitUpChair media network.
